What is an AI Toybox Photo Booth?

An AI Toybox photo booth represents the next evolution in event entertainment technology. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How long does it take to create each AI transformation?

The entire process takes approximately 30-60 seconds from photo capture to final digital delivery. Guests can see their transformation happening in real-time, making the wait part of the entertainment experience.


What file formats do guests receive?

All guests receive high-resolution PNG files optimized for social media sharing, along with print-ready versions suitable for physical output. Custom formats can be arranged based on specific event needs.


Can the AI photo booth work outdoors?

Yes, our systems include weatherproofing options and portable power solutions suitable for outdoor events. However, optimal lighting conditions produce the best results, so covered areas are preferred.


How many guests can use the photo booth per hour?

Under normal conditions, our AI photo booth systems can accommodate 40-60 guests per hour, depending on the complexity of chosen transformations and group size variations.


Is there an age limit for using the AI photo booth?

The technology works best with participants aged 5 and older. Younger children may need adult assistance, but the system can create wonderful results for guests of all ages.


What happens if the internet connection fails?

Our systems include offline processing capabilities and automatic cloud backup once connectivity resumes. Guests will still receive their digital creations regardless of temporary connection issues.


Can we customize the AI transformations for our specific event theme?

Absolutely! Custom transformation styles, backgrounds, and branding elements can be developed specifically for your event. Advanced notice allows our team to create truly unique experiences tailored to your vision.


Do guests need to download an app or create accounts?

No downloads or account creation required. Guests simply interact with the touchscreen interface and receive their digital creations via email, text, or QR code scanning.


What safety measures are in place for data privacy?

All facial data used for AI processing is deleted immediately after transformation completion. Only the final artistic creations are retained, and these are shared only with the guest's explicit consent.


How far in advance should we book?

Popular event dates, especially during peak seasons, book quickly. We recommend securing your AI photo booth rental at least 4-6 weeks in advance, though last-minute availability may exist depending on demand.
